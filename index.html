Green Pharmaceuticals

Towards Sustainable and Eco-Friendly Drug Development




Introduction

Green pharmaceuticus is the concept that integrates Green chemistry principles into the entire pharmaceutical lifecycle to minimize the environmental impact of medicine production,use, and disposal

Green pharmacy
concept focuses on:

Green Drug Design
• Designing drugs that degrade safely in the environment.
• Avoiding molecules that accumulate in water bodies or wildlife.
Sustainable Manufacturing
• Use of green solvents (e.g., water, ethanol) instead of toxic ones.
Drug Disposal
• Public education on safe disposal methods.

Strategies for
Green Pharma

  • Process Optimization (QbD) for less waste
  • Biocatalysis: Enzymes instead of toxic chemicals
  • Nanotechnology for targeted drug delivery

Management of Green
Pharmaceuticals

  • Green pharmacy prevents environmental contamination due to API (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient) present in the medicine.

Key Principles

Green Chemistry: Minimizing hazardous chemicals in drug synthesis
Eco-friendly Manufacturing: Reducing waste, energy use, and emission
Biodegradable Drugs: Designing medicines that break down safely in the environment
Sustainable Sourcing: Using renewable and plant-based materials
